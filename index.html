GR4NDM4

A Dashnet Discord Bot

GR4NDM4 is a moderation and social bot for Dashnet Forums made by The Wizard.

Command Help

<Parameter> is required

[Parameter] is optional

| refers to mutually exclusive arguments.

Tags

Custom, Configurable commands! Also works in DMs!

Tag

/tag <tag_name>

Sends a tag.

Aliases

/t

Wiki

/wiki <search_term>

Sends a wiki search result.

Aliases

/w /wikisearch

Tag List

/taglist [category]

Sends a list of all tags accessible to you, or in a category.

Aliases

/tl, /listtags /lt

Polls

Create and manage polls!

Poll

/poll <title>

Starts a Poll. It will ask you for more info.

Aliases

/p, /createpoll /createnewpoll /newpoll

Poll Status

/pollstatus [poll_name]

View the status of all active (public) polls, or a specified poll

Aliases

/ps, /pollstatus
